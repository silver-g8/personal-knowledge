
## Knowledge Flow

1. Capture raw material in `00-Inbox/`.
2. Move learning material into `02-Learning/`.
3. Turn repeated facts into `04-Reference/`.
4. Turn active work into `03-Projects/`.
5. Track long-term responsibilities in `05-Areas/`.
6. Move finished or obsolete material into `09-Archive/`.
