## Change Types

- `Added`: new content.
- `Changed`: existing content updated.
- `Fixed`: corrections.
- `Removed`: removed obsolete content.
- `Archived`: moved to Archive.
- `Refactored`: improved structure without changing meaning.
- `Linked`: added cross references.
- `Reviewed`: AI reviewed existing knowledge.

## Guidelines

- Keep entries concise.
- Use one logical change per entry.
- Use dates in `YYYY-MM-DD` format.
- Do not remove historical records.
- Archive instead of deleting.
